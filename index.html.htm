Frequently Asked Questions About Roofing Services

How Do I Know If I Need a New Roof?

If there are leaks, missing tiles, damaged flashing, etc. Or if there is possible damage from hail, even if they do not cause immediate leaks, it is time to request a review of your roof. Likewise if your roof is between 12 and 15 years old, it is best to have it evaluated by a professional.

How Often Should I Replace My Roof?

The quality of materials, installation and proper ventilation will all affect the longevity of your roofing system. The normal thing is that a roof has a useful life between 15 and 25 years depending on the area. Weather conditions and location can also have an impact on the life of your roof.

What Materials Should I Use?

The type of materials will depend on the position of your roof; the most common and have a wide variety of options are asphalt shingles. We are GAF and Certainteed certified and can offer you many different options.
